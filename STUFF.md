## Common Questions

**How do I borrow materials?**
- Log materials borrowed in the checkout sheet
- Return or replace within 30 days
- Small amounts (scraps, nails, etc.) are free

**Can I store my project here?**
- Short-term (under 2 weeks): Use labeled shelf space
- Long-term: Rent a project locker ($10/month)
- Never block aisles or work areas

**How do I get trained on power tools?**
- Sign up for safety training class
- Or schedule individual training with certified members
- List of certified trainers is on the bulletin board

**What if I break something?**
- Don't hide it! Report to shop manager immediately
- Accidents happen - honesty helps us maintain safe tools
- Negligence/misuse may result in repair costs or membership review
